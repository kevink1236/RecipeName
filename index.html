Recipe #1 (Cakes)

Time:

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 30 mins
  • Total: 50 mins

Servings:

  • 12 cupcakes
  • 1-9x9 inch cakes
Ingredients Quantity
White Sugar 1 Cup
Butter 1/2 Cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 2 teaspoons
All-purpose flour 1 1/2 cups
Baking powder 1 3/4 teaspoons
Milk 1/2 cup

Directions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan or line a muffin pan with paper liners.

Step 2: In a medium bowl, cream together the sugar and butter. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Combine flour and baking powder, add tot eh creamed mixture and mix well. Finally stir in the milk until batter is smooth. Pour or spoon batter into the prepared pan.

Step 3: Bake for 30 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven. For cupcakes, bake 20 to 25 minutes. Cake is done when it springs back to the touch.

Source: Simple White Cake

Recipe #2 (Pancakes)

Time:

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 15 mins

Servings:

  • 4 pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
All purpose Flour 1 cup
White sugar 2 Tablespoons
Baking powder 2 Teaspoons
Salt 1 Teaspoon
Egg, beaten 1
Vegetable oil 2 Tablespoons

Directions:

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.

Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using apoorixmately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.

Source: Easy Pancakes

Recipe #3 (Brownies)

Time:

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 20 mins

Servings:

  • 16 Brownies
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 Cup
White sugar 1 Cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 Teaspoon
Unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 Cup
All-purpose flour 1/2 Cup
Salt 1/4 Teaspoon
Baking powder 1/4 Teaspoon

Frosting:

Ingredients Quantity
Softened butter 3 Tablespoons
Unsweetened cocoa powder 3 Tablespoons
Honey 1 Tablespoons
Vanilla extract 3 Teaspoon
Confectioners' sugar 1 Cup

Directions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

Step 2: In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

Step 4: To make frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cupt confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.

Srouce: Best Brownies
